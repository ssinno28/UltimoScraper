Leagues - Indoor Soccer

  Session # 4 Begins: 4/29/19 Ends: 6/27/19

  • NO PLAYER MEMBERSHIP FEES or additional referee fees.
  • MORE GAMES: Our leagues include 7 league games plus up to 2 playoff games

Session #4 of indoor soccer will offer league play for men's, and women's teams in the open, O-30, O-40 and O-50 age levels. All adult teams will be ranked and then bracketed according to relative competitive levels.
For the first time, we are offering a High School age coed league and an Over 50 Men's league.  Please note game days when registering.  The Hish School coed league will play 8 games, no playoffs. 

7 game dates for all Adult teams plus playoffs for the top 4 teams in each bracket will be scheduled. Play will be 6 v 6 on our new synthetic grass lined soccer field where players can experience outdoor grass field play only indoors year round in our controlled climate building.

Our synthetic grass surface looks and plays like an outdoor field, right down to the players wearing molded cleats on it.

Schedules, scores and standings will be posted online and available to you any time of the day or night via our web site. Information will be updated minimum of twice each week.
Online league registration and payment for our customers is available. In order to reserve and confirm your space in our league, a Non-Refundable $100 deposit must be made. You may pay only the deposit, or the whole league fee online via a Master,Visa or Discover Credit Card (sorry no Amex cards accepted). Your transaction will be handled through Verisign. We also offer traditional "offline" payment methods via CC or personal check or cash. Any balances must be paid in full prior to the first game of the season and may be done through our payment center on this website or at our facility in person or over the phone. Rosters may have up to 25 players. (Note: maximum number of awards for playoffs will be 15).

For additional questions or information, please email Mike Rugnetta at miker@fake-site.com
