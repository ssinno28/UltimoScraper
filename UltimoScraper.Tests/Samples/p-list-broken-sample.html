  5v5 USFF Futsal Soccer League
  Fake Site 5v5 fast footwork futsal league is now a USFF affiliated league and will be played on our two professional Taraflex surface futsal fields. This league will be played in a 5v5 format with futsal referees and futsal soccer rules on an area 75x100 on a special surface with specially sized nets and a lower bounce futsal ball. Our professional Taraflex surface is used in international indoor soccer and futsal competitions and are the only courts of their kind in the New England area. This league offers an excellent opportunity for a very fast game with no boards that demands a lot from players, young and old, and can greatly enhance individual player development. As with all of our leagues, convenient online registration and payments, online schedules, and online scores and standings will be maintained and posted daily by Fake Site.
