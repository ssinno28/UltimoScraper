For this coming fall and winter, Fake Site is offering two 9 game sessions (Session 1-2: Nov. 1, 2018 to Jan. 21, 2019 and Session 2-3: Jan. 22, 2019 to Apr. 14, 2019. Each session will include a minimum of 9 scheduled games plus playoffs for the top 4 teams or top 50% of teams in bracket.

In our first session of indoor soccer, we are offering league play for Premier (select and strong club teams), A level (Weaker club, Div. 1 and 2 travel teams), and B level (Div. 3 and town recreational teams)for Boys & Girls U8-U18, Coed HS U19, Men's, Women's & Adult Coed teams in the open, O-30 and O-40 age brackets. Please note, all Coed teams need to have a minimum of 2 women players on field at all times.

Youth teams should indicate their age and requested playing level along with additional information (outdoor leagues, records, etc) to help in the proper placement of team by Fake Site. All teams within each two year bracket (U8/9, U9/10, U11/12, U13/14) will be ranked by Fake Site from top to bottom given the information provided along with other sources, and then placed into appropriate competitive sections (minimum of 4 teams, max of 8 teams per section). The birthday cutoff date for each age level is January, 1, 2018 (ie: a U10 player must be born on or after January 1, 2009). MYSA Age Group Link.

Play will be a mix of 6v6 (7v7 for U10 and younger) boarded and unboarded soccer on our state of the art indoor synthetic grass boarded arena and our 2 lined (no boards) synthetic grass fields. Fields are 60 yards long by 30 yards wide. Schedules, scores and standings will be posted weekly online for viewing via our web site. Individual player awards will be presented to the two finalist teams in each section.

Online Rosters/Waivers need to be completed by the FIRST game, and may have up to 18 players (Note: maximum number of awards for playoffs will be 15). Changes to rosters can be made through the 4th game of season. All teams are responsible for providing a valid contact email address as this will be the primary method of communication with all teams prior to and during the season.
